## ğŸ” Component Breakdown

### Technical Content (8 marks - 40%)
**What:** Depth and accuracy of technical information

**Evaluates:**
- Understanding of concepts
- Accuracy of information
- Recent developments (2022+)
- Critical analysis
- Original insights

**Mark Ranges:**
- 7.5-8: Outstanding
- 6-7: Very Good
- 4-5.5: Fair
- 0-3.5: Poor

---

### Overall Quality (5 marks - 25%)
**What:** Language and presentation quality

**Evaluates:**
- Grammar and language
- Professional writing style
- Clarity and readability
- Consistent terminology
- Error-free presentation

**Mark Ranges:**
- 4.5-5: Outstanding
- 3.5-4: Very Good
- 2-3: Fair
- 0-1.5: Poor

---

### Templates Followed (4 marks - 20%)
**What:** Adherence to format and structure

**Evaluates:**
- Following prescribed template
- All required sections present
- Consistent formatting
- Proper numbering
- Professional layout

**Mark Ranges:**
- 3.5-4: Outstanding
- 2.5-3: Very Good
- 1.5-2: Fair
- 0-1: Poor

---

### Adequacy of References (3 marks - 15%)
**What:** Quality and citation of sources

**Evaluates:**
- 2-3 quality references
- Recent papers (2022+)
- Indexed journals/tier-1 conferences
- Proper citation format
- No plagiarism

**Mark Ranges:**
- 2.5-3: Outstanding
- 2-2.5: Very Good
- 1-1.5: Fair
- 0-0.5: Poor

## ğŸ“ Usage Examples

### For Students Preparing Report

**Step 1:** Read Quick Reference
- Understand what's expected
- Note the 4 components and their weightage

**Step 2:** Plan Your Report
- Allocate effort based on weightage (40-25-20-15)
- Focus most on Technical Content (40%)

**Step 3:** Self-Evaluate
- Use the checklist before submission
- Aim for "Outstanding" level in each component

**Step 4:** Finalize
- Proofread for quality
- Check template adherence
- Verify all citations

---

### For Evaluators Marking Reports

**Step 1:** Review Rubrics
- Understand criteria for each component
- Familiarize with mark ranges

**Step 2:** Use Evaluation Sheet
- Fill in marks for each of 4 components
- Refer to Quick Reference as needed

**Step 3:** Calculate Total
- TC(8) + OQ(5) + TF(4) + AR(3) = Report Total (20)

**Step 4:** Provide Feedback
- Note strengths and areas for improvement
- Use specific criteria from rubrics
