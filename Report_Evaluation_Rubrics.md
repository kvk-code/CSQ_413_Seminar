# Report Evaluation Rubrics and Marking Scheme

## Overview
This document provides a detailed breakdown of the Report Evaluation component (20 marks) for the CSQ413 Seminar course. The evaluation is based on four key criteria: Technical Content, Overall Quality, Templates Followed, and Adequacy of References.

## Total Marks: 20

### Detailed Splitup

#### 1. Technical Content (8 marks)

**Description:** Evaluates the depth, accuracy, and relevance of the technical information presented in the report.

| Performance Level | Criteria | Marks |
|------------------|----------|-------|
| **Outstanding** | - In-depth coverage of the topic with comprehensive technical details<br>- Demonstrates thorough understanding of concepts<br>- Includes recent developments and state-of-the-art approaches<br>- Critical analysis and synthesis of information from multiple sources<br>- Original insights and inferences clearly presented | 7.5 - 8 |
| **Very Good** | - Good coverage of technical content<br>- Shows clear understanding of major concepts<br>- Includes relevant technical details and methodologies<br>- Some analysis and comparison of different approaches<br>- Most concepts explained clearly | 6 - 7 |
| **Fair** | - Basic technical content present<br>- Limited depth in technical explanations<br>- Relies heavily on single source or lacks breadth<br>- Minimal analysis or critical thinking<br>- Some technical inaccuracies present | 4 - 5.5 |
| **Poor** | - Superficial technical content<br>- Lacks understanding of fundamental concepts<br>- Significant technical errors or misunderstandings<br>- Copied content without comprehension<br>- Irrelevant or outdated information | 0 - 3.5 |

#### 2. Overall Quality (5 marks)

**Description:** Assesses the overall presentation quality, language, readability, and professional appearance of the report.

| Performance Level | Criteria | Marks |
|------------------|----------|-------|
| **Outstanding** | - Excellent language with no grammatical errors<br>- Professional writing style throughout<br>- Clear, concise, and well-structured sentences<br>- Consistent terminology and notation<br>- Highly readable and engaging<br>- No spelling or typographical errors | 4.5 - 5 |
| **Very Good** | - Good language quality with minor grammatical issues<br>- Generally professional writing style<br>- Clear sentence structure<br>- Mostly consistent terminology<br>- Good readability<br>- Few spelling errors | 3.5 - 4 |
| **Fair** | - Acceptable language with multiple grammatical errors<br>- Inconsistent writing style<br>- Some unclear or ambiguous sentences<br>- Inconsistent terminology usage<br>- Moderate readability issues<br>- Several spelling/typographical errors | 2 - 3 |
| **Poor** | - Poor language quality with frequent errors<br>- Unprofessional presentation<br>- Difficult to read and understand<br>- Inconsistent and confusing terminology<br>- Numerous spelling and grammatical errors<br>- Lacks coherence | 0 - 1.5 |

#### 3. Templates Followed (4 marks)

**Description:** Evaluates adherence to the prescribed report template, formatting standards, and structural organization.

| Performance Level | Criteria | Marks |
|------------------|----------|-------|
| **Outstanding** | - Perfect adherence to prescribed template<br>- All required sections present and properly organized<br>- Consistent formatting throughout (fonts, spacing, margins)<br>- Proper page numbering and headers/footers<br>- All diagrams, figures, and tables properly numbered and captioned<br>- Table of contents, list of figures/tables included<br>- Professional layout and presentation | 3.5 - 4 |
| **Very Good** | - Follows template with minor deviations<br>- All major sections present<br>- Generally consistent formatting<br>- Most figures and tables properly numbered<br>- Includes required front matter<br>- Good overall organization | 2.5 - 3 |
| **Fair** | - Partially follows template<br>- Some required sections missing or incomplete<br>- Inconsistent formatting in places<br>- Figures/tables not consistently numbered or captioned<br>- Missing some required components<br>- Organization needs improvement | 1.5 - 2 |
| **Poor** | - Does not follow prescribed template<br>- Major sections missing<br>- Inconsistent or inappropriate formatting<br>- Figures and tables not properly numbered<br>- Missing critical components<br>- Poor organization and structure | 0 - 1 |

#### 4. Adequacy of References (3 marks)

**Description:** Evaluates the quality, relevance, and proper citation of references used in the report.

| Performance Level | Criteria | Marks |
|------------------|----------|-------|
| **Outstanding** | - Minimum 2-3 high-quality references from indexed journals/tier-1 conferences<br>- References are recent (not earlier than 2022)<br>- Appropriate mix: max one survey/review paper, others methodology/results papers<br>- All citations properly formatted (IEEE/standard format)<br>- All references cited in text and listed in bibliography<br>- No uncited references in bibliography<br>- All sources are credible and peer-reviewed | 2.5 - 3 |
| **Very Good** | - Adequate number of quality references (2-3)<br>- Most references are recent and relevant<br>- Good mix of survey and research papers<br>- Citations mostly properly formatted<br>- Minor inconsistencies in citation format<br>- All major sources cited properly | 2 - 2.5 |
| **Fair** | - Minimum references present but quality questionable<br>- Some outdated references (before 2022)<br>- Improper mix of reference types<br>- Inconsistent citation format<br>- Some uncited content or missing citations<br>- Some non-credible sources included | 1 - 1.5 |
| **Poor** | - Insufficient number of references<br>- Poor quality or non-credible sources<br>- Outdated references<br>- No proper citation format followed<br>- Plagiarized or unacknowledged content<br>- Missing bibliography or reference list | 0 - 0.5 |

## Guidelines for Evaluators

### Technical Content (8 marks)
- Look for depth of understanding, not just breadth
- Check if concepts are explained in the student's own words
- Verify if recent developments (2022 onwards) are included
- Assess critical thinking and analysis
- Check for technical accuracy

### Overall Quality (5 marks)
- Evaluate language proficiency and grammar
- Assess readability and flow
- Check for consistency in style and tone
- Look for professional presentation
- Verify absence of errors

### Templates Followed (4 marks)
- Verify all required sections are present:
  - Title page
  - Abstract
  - Table of contents
  - Introduction
  - Main content sections
  - Conclusion
  - References
  - List of figures/tables (if applicable)
- Check formatting consistency (fonts, spacing, margins)
- Verify proper numbering of sections, figures, and tables
- Ensure professional layout

### Adequacy of References (3 marks)
- Minimum 2-3 references required
- Maximum one survey/review paper
- References should be from:
  - Indexed journals (SCI, Scopus, etc.)
  - Tier-1 conferences
  - Not earlier than 2022
- Check citation format (IEEE or as specified)
- Verify all in-text citations have corresponding bibliography entries
- Ensure no plagiarism

## Common Issues to Watch For

### Technical Content
- ❌ Copied content without understanding
- ❌ Superficial treatment of topic
- ❌ Technical inaccuracies
- ❌ Outdated information
- ✅ Original insights and analysis
- ✅ Comprehensive coverage
- ✅ Accurate technical details

### Overall Quality
- ❌ Poor grammar and spelling
- ❌ Inconsistent terminology
- ❌ Difficult to read
- ✅ Professional writing style
- ✅ Clear and concise
- ✅ Well-structured paragraphs

### Templates Followed
- ❌ Missing required sections
- ❌ Inconsistent formatting
- ❌ Unnumbered figures/tables
- ✅ Complete structure
- ✅ Consistent formatting
- ✅ Professional appearance

### Adequacy of References
- ❌ Insufficient references
- ❌ Outdated papers (pre-2022)
- ❌ Non-credible sources
- ❌ Missing citations
- ✅ Quality peer-reviewed sources
- ✅ Recent publications
- ✅ Proper citation format

## Example Evaluation Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Outstanding Report (18-20 marks)
- Technical Content: 7.5 marks (comprehensive, accurate, analytical)
- Overall Quality: 4.5 marks (excellent language, professional)
- Templates Followed: 4 marks (perfect adherence)
- Adequacy of References: 3 marks (3 quality papers, properly cited)
- **Total: 19 marks**

### Scenario 2: Very Good Report (15-17 marks)
- Technical Content: 6.5 marks (good coverage, minor gaps)
- Overall Quality: 4 marks (good language, few errors)
- Templates Followed: 3 marks (follows template, minor deviations)
- Adequacy of References: 2.5 marks (adequate, mostly proper)
- **Total: 16 marks**

### Scenario 3: Fair Report (10-14 marks)
- Technical Content: 5 marks (basic content, limited depth)
- Overall Quality: 3 marks (acceptable, some errors)
- Templates Followed: 2 marks (partial adherence)
- Adequacy of References: 1.5 marks (minimum references, some issues)
- **Total: 11.5 marks**

### Scenario 4: Poor Report (0-9 marks)
- Technical Content: 3 marks (superficial, inaccurate)
- Overall Quality: 2 marks (poor language, many errors)
- Templates Followed: 1 mark (doesn't follow template)
- Adequacy of References: 0.5 marks (insufficient, poor quality)
- **Total: 6.5 marks**

## Notes for Students

1. **Start Early**: Begin working on the report alongside your presentation preparation
2. **Follow Template**: Strictly adhere to the prescribed template
3. **Quality References**: Choose recent (2022+), peer-reviewed papers from reputed sources
4. **Proper Citations**: Use consistent citation format (IEEE recommended)
5. **Original Work**: Write in your own words demonstrating understanding
6. **Proofread**: Check for grammar, spelling, and formatting errors
7. **Seek Feedback**: Get your guide's input before final submission
8. **Technical Accuracy**: Verify all technical details and concepts

## Submission Requirements

- Submit both **hard copy** and **soft copy**
- Hard copy should be professionally bound
- Soft copy should be uploaded to Google Classroom
- Include all referenced papers (hard and soft copies)
- Ensure guide has verified and signed the report
- Submit by the end of semester deadline
