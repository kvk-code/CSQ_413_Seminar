# Quick Reference: Report Evaluation Splitup (20 Marks)

## Summary Table

| # | Component | Marks | % | Key Focus Areas |
|---|-----------|-------|---|-----------------|
| 1 | **Technical Content** | 8 | 40% | Depth, accuracy, understanding, analysis |
| 2 | **Overall Quality** | 5 | 25% | Language, readability, presentation |
| 3 | **Templates Followed** | 4 | 20% | Format, structure, organization |
| 4 | **Adequacy of References** | 3 | 15% | Quality, recency, proper citations |
| | **TOTAL** | **20** | **100%** | |

## Detailed Breakdown

### 1. Technical Content (8 marks) - 40%

**What to evaluate:**
- Depth of technical understanding
- Accuracy of information
- Coverage of recent developments (2022+)
- Critical analysis and synthesis
- Original insights and inferences

**Marking guideline:**
- **7.5-8**: Outstanding - Comprehensive, accurate, analytical
- **6-7**: Very Good - Good coverage, clear understanding
- **4-5.5**: Fair - Basic content, limited depth
- **0-3.5**: Poor - Superficial, inaccurate

### 2. Overall Quality (5 marks) - 25%

**What to evaluate:**
- Language quality and grammar
- Professional writing style
- Clarity and readability
- Consistency in terminology
- Absence of spelling/typographical errors

**Marking guideline:**
- **4.5-5**: Outstanding - Excellent language, professional
- **3.5-4**: Very Good - Good quality, minor issues
- **2-3**: Fair - Acceptable, multiple errors
- **0-1.5**: Poor - Poor language, difficult to read

### 3. Templates Followed (4 marks) - 20%

**What to evaluate:**
- Adherence to prescribed template
- Presence of all required sections
- Consistent formatting (fonts, spacing, margins)
- Proper numbering of figures, tables, equations
- Professional layout

**Required sections:**
- Title page
- Abstract
- Table of contents
- Introduction
- Main content sections
- Conclusion
- References
- List of figures/tables (if applicable)

**Marking guideline:**
- **3.5-4**: Outstanding - Perfect adherence
- **2.5-3**: Very Good - Follows with minor deviations
- **1.5-2**: Fair - Partial adherence
- **0-1**: Poor - Does not follow template

### 4. Adequacy of References (3 marks) - 15%

**What to evaluate:**
- Number: Minimum 2-3 references
- Quality: Indexed journals/tier-1 conferences
- Recency: Not earlier than 2022
- Mix: Max one survey/review paper
- Citation format: Proper and consistent
- No plagiarism

**Marking guideline:**
- **2.5-3**: Outstanding - Quality references, properly cited
- **2-2.5**: Very Good - Adequate references, mostly proper
- **1-1.5**: Fair - Minimum references, some issues
- **0-0.5**: Poor - Insufficient or poor quality

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Technical Content ❌
- Copying without understanding
- Superficial treatment
- Technical errors
- Outdated information

### Overall Quality ❌
- Poor grammar and spelling
- Unprofessional style
- Difficult to read
- Inconsistent terminology

### Templates Followed ❌
- Missing sections
- Inconsistent formatting
- Unnumbered figures/tables
- Poor organization

### References ❌
- Insufficient number (<2)
- Outdated papers (pre-2022)
- Poor quality sources
- Missing citations
- Improper format

## For Students: How to Score Well

### Technical Content (8 marks)
1. Read and understand papers thoroughly
2. Write in your own words
3. Include recent developments
4. Show critical thinking
5. Add your own insights

### Overall Quality (5 marks)
1. Use professional language
2. Proofread carefully
3. Maintain consistent style
4. Keep it clear and concise
5. Check grammar and spelling

### Templates Followed (4 marks)
1. Follow prescribed template strictly
2. Include all required sections
3. Use consistent formatting
4. Number all figures and tables
5. Create professional layout

### Adequacy of References (3 marks)
1. Choose 2-3 quality papers
2. Ensure papers are recent (2022+)
3. Use proper citation format (IEEE)
4. Include mix of paper types
5. Cite all sources properly
